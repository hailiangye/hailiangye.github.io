Group Members

    Professors

  • Prof. Feilong Cao


    • He received the Ph.D. degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University, Xi'an, China, in 2003. From 2004 to 2006, he was a Post-Doctoral Research Fellow with the School of Aerospace, Xi'an Jiaotong University. From June 2011 to December 2011 and October 2013 to January 2014, he was a Visiting Professor with the Department of Computer Science, Chonbuk National University, South Korea, and the Department of Computer Sciences and Computer Engineering, LaTrobe University, Melbourne, Australia, respectively. From 2004, he is a Professor with China Jiliang University, Hangzhou, China, and was the dean of the College of Sciences from 2008 to 2017. He is also the Distinguished Professor of Northwest University, Xi'an, China and the Helan Mountains Scholarship with Ningxia University, Yinchuan, China, respectively. Dr. Cao has headed eight National Natural Science Foundations of China and authored or co-authored over 300 scientific papers in refereed journals and conferences, including IEEE TPAMI, AI, IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TIP, IEEE TCSVT, IEEE TGAS, IEEE TSNE, IEEE TETCI, PR, ICML, etc. His current research interests include artificial neural networks theory, deep learning, graph neural networks, point cloud analysis, and image processing.
