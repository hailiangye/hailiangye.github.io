Biography

I am an Associate Professor at the Department of Applied Mathematics, College of Sciences, China Jiliang University, Hangzhou, China. I received the M.Sc. degree in applied mathematics from China Jiliang University, Hangzhou, China, in 2015, advised by Prof. Feilong Cao, and the Ph.D. degree in Computational Mathematics from the Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, China, in 2019, advised by Prof. Hong Li. I am the Young Outstanding Talents of "Zhejiang Province University Leading Talents Training Program"(China). From 2020, I am the Director of Data Modeling and Intelligent Computing Research Team of China Jiliang University. From February 2024 to August 2024, he was a Visiting Fellow with the School of Mathematical & Physical Sciences, Macquarie University, Sydney, Australia. I have authored or co-authored about 60 scientific papers in refereed journals, such as IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TCYB, IEEE TIP, IEEE TGRS, IEEE TETCI, IEEE TSNE, IEEE JHBI, Pattern Recognition, Neural Networks, Information Processing & Management, Information Sciences, Knowledge-Based Systems, Expert Systems with Applications, etc.

I am a member of IEEE, a member of China Computer Federation (CCF), a member of Chinese Association for Artificial Intelligence (CAAI), and a member of Chinese Society for Industrial and Applied Mathematics (CSIAM). I am a regular reviewer and has reviewed more than 100 papers for top journals, including IEEE TIP, IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TCYB, IEEE TCSVT, IEEE TGRS, Neural Networks, KBS, Applied Intelligence, EAAI, ESWA, IJMLC, etc. Moreover, I am an Associate Editor of Soft Computing (Springer).
